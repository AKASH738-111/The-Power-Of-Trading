Organic Insecticide

Benefits:

Why Organic Better Than Chemicals

Chemicals: destroy microbes, harden soil, create salt buildup and nutrient lock. Every year dose increase karna padta hai.

Organic inputs: soil microbes ko food dete hai, humus badhate hai, soil ko soft banate hai, water holding improve karte hai aur nutrients naturally release karte hai. Soil fertility season by season strong hoti hai.
