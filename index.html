Why Organic is Better Than Chemicals

Chemical Farming:
• Kills soil microbes
• Hardens soil structure
• Causes nutrient lock
• Needs increasing doses every year

Organic Farming:
• Builds beneficial microbes
• Improves soil porosity & water holding
• Naturally releases nutrients
• Fertility increases year by year

Organic inputs act like food for soil life. Microbes convert nutrients into plant-available forms. Over time soil becomes soft, fertile, and self-sustaining — unlike chemical farming where soil becomes dependent on fertilizers.
