About The Power Of Trading

The Power Of Trading was created with a single vision — to remove confusion, emotions, and randomness from trading and replace it with logic, data, discipline and consistency.

Founded by Akash Rajput, a professional trader and Data Engineer with 7+ years of experience in the Indian Stock & Forex Market. Akash has personally mentored over 1000+ students into professional traders.

This platform is unique because it combines technology, market psychology, institutional strategy, and real data analysis to teach trading as a business. Students learn step-by-step how smart money moves, price action, and risk management, not just indicators or shortcuts.

Students who complete this program are able to:

  • Analyze charts professionally and identify high-probability trades
  • Understand institutional market flows and FII/DII activities
  • Trade Forex and Indian Stock Markets confidently
  • Develop discipline, mindset & consistency
  • Use data-engineered systems even as beginners

The curriculum includes market basics, technicals, fundamentals, price action, candlestick psychology, risk control, trade execution, and advanced professional strategies. This is one of the few platforms where students can actually learn to earn professionally in India.

By reading this section carefully, a new student can spend 30–60 minutes understanding the depth of our approach and methodology.
