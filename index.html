Ultra Organic Growth Booster

Ultra is a microbial-rich plant tonic designed to enhance chlorophyll production, activate plant enzymes and improve nutrient assimilation. Unlike chemical growth promoters which push plants artificially, this tonic strengthens the plant’s own metabolism, making growth natural and long-lasting.

It improves leaf expansion, stem thickness and root respiration. Continuous use leads to better drought tolerance, faster recovery after stress and higher flowering capacity.

Main Benefits
  • Boosts photosynthesis efficiency
  • Improves leaf size & dark green colour
  • Strengthens plant immunity
  • Increases final yield potential

Nemark Organic Bio-Pesticide

Nemark is a neem-based organic insect control formulation that disrupts pest feeding behaviour, egg laying cycle and larval development. Unlike chemical pesticides that kill instantly but damage soil biology, Nemark works through repellent, anti-feeding and growth-inhibition mechanisms.

It protects beneficial insects, improves plant resistance and prevents future infestation cycles, resulting in sustainable pest control.

Main Benefits
  • Controls aphids, thrips, whiteflies & sucking pests
  • Breaks pest breeding cycle
  • Safe for soil microbes & pollinators
  • Improves crop immunity naturally

Ultra Organic Fungicide

This organic fungicide works by strengthening plant defense enzymes and stimulating beneficial rhizosphere microbes that suppress fungal growth. While chemical fungicides kill fungus temporarily, they also destroy soil micro-life. This formulation restores soil balance while protecting crops.

Regular application improves root oxygen flow, prevents leaf infections, reduces rot conditions and improves nutrient uptake efficiency.

Main Benefits
  • Controls leaf spot, mildew & root rot
  • Enhances beneficial soil microbes
  • Improves root respiration
  • Long-term disease resistance
