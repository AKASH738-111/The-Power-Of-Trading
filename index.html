About The Power Of Trading

The Power Of Trading is not just a course or a learning platform — it is a complete trading ecosystem built with the vision of making trading simple, logical, and profitable for every individual, even for those who have zero prior market knowledge.

This platform was founded by Akash Rajput, a professional trader and Data Engineer with over 7+ years of solid experience in the Indian Stock Market as well as the International Forex Market. Over the years, Akash observed one common problem in the trading industry — most people lose money not because trading is risky, but because they lack a structured, data-driven system.

Unlike traditional trading educators who focus only on indicators or theory, The Power Of Trading is built on real market data, statistical probability, and technology-driven decision making. Akash Rajput used his background in Data Engineering to design a unique trading system where even non-technical people can understand market movements and execute trades confidently.

The goal of this platform is very clear — to eliminate confusion, emotional trading, and blind tips. Students are trained to understand market structure, institutional activity, price behavior, and risk management in such a way that trading becomes a professional skill, not gambling.

Till date, more than 1000+ students from different states of India have been mentored through this system. Many of them started as complete beginners and are now consistently executing trades with discipline and confidence.

The Power Of Trading is one of the very few platforms in India that combines market experience + data science + automation logic. This allows traders to follow a repeatable system instead of emotional decision making. Our long-term vision is to make professional trading accessible from home, without dependency on tips, calls, or luck.
