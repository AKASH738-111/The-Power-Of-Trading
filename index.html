About The Power Of Trading

The Power Of Trading is not just another trading course — it is a **complete learning ecosystem** designed for people who want clarity, structure and long-term consistency in financial markets.

This platform was founded by Akash Rajput, a professional trader with 7+ years of deep experience across the Indian stock market and the global Forex market. Like most traders, Akash’s journey began with confusion, emotional trading and losses. But instead of quitting, he chose to study markets from their core — using data, logic and probability.

Apart from trading, Akash is also a Data Engineer. This technical background gave him a unique advantage — he started observing markets the same way engineers analyze systems. He studied price behavior, institutional money flow, volume patterns, time-based movements and trader psychology.

After years of research, testing and real-market execution, he built a rule-based trading framework that removes guesswork. This system allows even beginners — people who have never traded before — to understand when to trade, when not to trade and how to manage risk professionally.

Till today, 1000+ students from different parts of India have been personally mentored. Many of them were beginners, working professionals or people who had previously failed in trading. With the right structure and mindset, they learned to treat trading like a business — not gambling.

The Power Of Trading is one of the very few platforms in India where technology, discipline and market psychology come together. Our mission is simple:

✔ Remove fear from trading
✔ Teach logic instead of tips
✔ Build independent traders, not dependent followers
✔ Create long-term consistency, not short-term excitement

If you are serious about learning how markets actually work, this platform is built for you.
