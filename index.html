About The Power Of Trading

The Power Of Trading was created with a single vision — to remove confusion, emotions, and randomness from trading and replace it with logic, data, discipline and consistency.

This institute is founded by Akash Rajput, who carries more than 7+ years of deep experience in the Indian Stock Market as well as the International Forex Market.

Unlike traditional mentors who teach indicators blindly, Akash Rajput focuses on how big institutions, banks and smart money actually operate in the market.

Being a professional Data Engineer, Akash Rajput has used real-world technology, statistics and automation concepts to design a rule-based trading system where even a beginner can trade with clarity.

Till date, more than 1000+ students from across India have been trained through this structured system — many of whom started with zero market knowledge.

This is one of the very few platforms in India where trading is taught as a business, not gambling, not tips, not shortcuts.
