About The Power Of Trading


The Power Of Trading is not just a trading institute — it is a complete mindset transformation platform. Our mission is to convert beginners into disciplined, professional traders who understand market psychology, institutional money flow, risk management and consistency.

Founded by Akash Rajput, a trader with over 7+ years of real market experience, this platform focuses on logic-based trading rather than gambling.

Students are trained deeply in:

  • ✔ Market Structure & Institutional Footprints
  • ✔ Price Action & Candlestick Psychology
  • ✔ Risk Management & Capital Protection
  • ✔ Emotional Discipline & Trader Psychology
  • ✔ Live Market Execution Framework

Our belief is simple: One good trade system + discipline = financial freedom.
