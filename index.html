About The Power Of Trading

The Power Of Trading is India’s most advanced trading education platform combining data science, market psychology, and technology to help beginners become professional traders.

Founded by Akash Rajput, a Data Engineer & trader with 7+ years experience in Indian & Forex markets, he has mentored 1000+ students into disciplined, confident traders.

Using proprietary systems, even students with zero prior knowledge can analyze charts, identify trends, and trade confidently. Courses focus on risk management, price action, and institutional logic.

Our mission: Build traders who can consistently earn, understand market psychology, and execute trades professionally from anywhere in India.

We provide live support, real-time data analysis, and mentorship so that each student gains clarity, discipline, and confidence in trading. The platform is designed for long-term consistency and professional mindset development.
